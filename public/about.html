About Simple Weather

This weather app aims to provide data about your chosen city location, as well as provide an option to save favorite weather data.

Accurate weather information is a crucial element to consider when planning a trip or visiting an area. This weather app provides a simple way to search for the current weather status, and helps you save your favorite location spots for later.

Key Features

  • Search by city:
    • Get current weather status, longitude, and latitude
    • See the coordinates as a visualized chart
  • Save favorites:
    • Save your favorite city, location, and weather type
    • Access your peviously saved favorites
