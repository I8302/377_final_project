Welcome to Simple Weather

A Weather Forecast solution that provides accurate weather data for your chosen location. You are able to input a city location, and get results about the current weather status, its longitude, and latitude. Additionally, you are able to input your favorites, save them for later, and view your saved (this includes city, country, and weather type).

To get started, simple head on over to the "Weather" tab. Happy Searching!
